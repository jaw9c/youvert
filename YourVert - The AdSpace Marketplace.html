Intro...

YouVert is the "Airbnb for Advertising Space". With the online AdSpace world being so saturated, physical AdSpace is a brilliant opportunity often overlooked by companies looking to get their name out there.

Why use YouVert?

YouVert gives individuals and organisations a great opportunity to easily monetise their previously useless space.

Being able to find the space which suits you best along with the unique and creative space that the YouVert community provides will ensure impactful advertising. YouVert provides the same benefits of an advertising agency but for a fraction of the price.

How It Works

Listing AdSpace

  1. Lister’s will create a profile to introduce and verify yourself.
  2. Describe the AdSpace labelling it with tags that will allow YouVert to find who the space will be appropriate for.
  3. Outline what makes the space unique to entice renter’s in.
  4. Start making money!

Renting AdSpace

  1. Renter’s also create a profile introducing themselves so YouVert can suggest which AdSpace will be most effective
  2. Pay a monthly fee to get access to a set amount of AdSpace
  3. “Pick ’n’ mix” the AdSpace you want from YouVert’s suggested space.

Our Aim - Be Creative

We want people to be innovative when sharing their space. Advertising is all about standing out so being creative with your AdSpace is a sure way to get people using it. There are millions of opportunities out there for you to get your name into the admosphere.
